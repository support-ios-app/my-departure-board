The board, before you leave the house

A home-screen widget with the live departure board for the stations you use — the same data as the screens on the platform, checked with a glance instead of opening an app. If the 14:10 is cancelled, you know while your coffee is still hot.

Only the trains you'd actually catch

Pin a commute — “from Waterloo, calling at Richmond” — and the board filters to trains that stop where you get off. No scrolling past every stopping service to Southampton to find yours.

Delayed? That can be money back.

Save your trip with one tap as you board. CommuteWatch checks how the train actually ran, and when the delay crosses your operator's Delay Repay threshold it shows you the amount of delay recorded and takes you straight to the claim form — with the journey details you need already on screen.

Especially useful if you pay by contactless or hold a season ticket — the passengers the automatic-compensation schemes mostly miss.
