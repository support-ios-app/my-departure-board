My Departure Board — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 July 2026

My Departure Board (“the app”) shows live UK National Rail departure information. This policy explains what data the app processes and why. We do not sell your data and we do not use it for advertising or cross-app tracking.

Who we are

The developer of My Departure Board. Contact: support.ios.app@gmail.com.

What we collect and why

  • Anonymous app identifier. On first launch the app creates an anonymous account (a random ID via Firebase Authentication). It is not linked to your name, email, or any real-world identity. We use it only to store the stations you save and your device’s notification registration.
  • Your saved commutes. The stations and filters you save, so we can show them to you.
  • Trip records (optional). If you use “Save today's trip”, we store the journey you recorded — date, origin and destination station, scheduled departure time, and operator — so the app can later show you how that train actually ran and whether the delay may qualify for the operator's Delay Repay scheme. Trip records are created only when you explicitly save a trip. You can delete individual records or all of them at any time in the app (Settings → My Trips & Delays), and they are deleted automatically after 12 months. We only show you factual delay information and a link to the operator's claim page — we never submit claims on your behalf.
  • Notification token. If you enable notifications, a push token so we can alert you to changes. (Notifications may be disabled in the current version.)
  • Usage analytics. We use Google Analytics for Firebase to understand which features are used (e.g. opening the app, viewing a board). This includes standard analytics data such as app interactions, device model, OS version, and approximate region derived from IP address. We do not use the Advertising Identifier (IDFA) and do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • Service logs. Our servers log requests (station code, app/widget, the anonymous identifier, timestamp, IP address) to run and secure the service.

Location

The app uses your device location on your device only, to find nearby stations. Your precise location is never sent to or stored on our servers — the app only sends station codes. (Analytics and server logs may infer an approximate region from your IP address.)

Who we share data with

  • Google (Firebase / Google Cloud / Google Analytics): as our authentication, database, hosting and analytics provider.
  • National Rail Enquiries / Network Rail: we consume their open rail-data feeds; we do not send them your personal data.

We do not sell personal data or share it with advertisers or data brokers.

Data retention

Saved commutes persist until you delete them or delete the app’s data. Trip records are kept for 12 months and then deleted automatically (you can delete them sooner in the app at any time). Analytics and server logs are retained for a limited period for operational purposes.

Your rights (UK GDPR)

You can request deletion of your data by contacting support.ios.app@gmail.com. Because accounts are anonymous, please contact us from the app or include details to help us locate it.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy; the “last updated” date will change.
